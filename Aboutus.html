About Us

Our Origin Story ElectronizoMarket was founded out of a passion for technology and a desire to bring the latest innovations in electronic devices to consumers in a more accessible and personalized way. Our journey began in a small workshop, where a group of tech enthusiasts and professionals came together with a shared vision: to create a retail experience that not only offers cutting-edge products but also provides unparalleled customer support and expertise. The inception of ElectronizoMarket was driven by the recognition of a gap in the market for a retailer that could combine a diverse product range with a high level of customer care. We saw an opportunity to build a brand that customers could rely on for both the quality of its products and the quality of its service. Starting with a modest selection of mobile phones and laptops, we have grown to include a wide array of electronics, including computers, cameras, and various accessories, always keeping our customers' evolving needs at the forefront. Our dedication to staying ahead of technological trends and our commitment to rigorous product testing have been key drivers of our success. We believe that every device we offer should enhance our customers' lives, whether it’s for work, creativity, or staying connected with loved ones. This philosophy has guided us from our humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in the electronics retail industry. Today, ElectronizoMarket stands as a testament to our founders' original mission: to provide a shopping experience that is as dynamic and innovative as the technology we sell. Our team, composed of industry experts and tech aficionados, works tirelessly to ensure that each customer receives the best possible service, from in-depth product knowledge to after-sales support. We are proud of our origins and even more excited about the future, as we continue to innovate and expand our offerings, staying true to the core values that started it all.

To learn more about our great service, just take a look at the awards we’ve won over the years. We always stock the latest new handsets and keep our fingers on the pulse to provide the latest smartphone news - so you can choose a mobile that's right for your lifestyle!
